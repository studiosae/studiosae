Is this therapy or counseling?

We offer both. Counseling typically focuses on specific issues and short-term goals, while therapy explores deeper patterns and long-term personal growth. We customize our approach based on your needs.

Do you prescribe medication?

No, we do not prescribe medication. We focus on psychotherapeutic approaches like Hypnotherapy, CBT, and Mindfulness. If medication is needed, we can refer you to a trusted psychiatrist.

Are sessions confidential?

Yes, absolutely. Confidentiality is a cornerstone of our practice. What you share in sessions stays between you and your therapist, with the only exceptions being legal requirements regarding safety.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes, we offer secure online sessions for clients who prefer the comfort of their own home or live at a distance. In-person sessions are also available.

How do I know what service is right for me?

You don't need to know exactly what you need before you start. During our initial consultation, we will discuss your challenges and goals to recommend the best approach for you.

Do you provide referrals to psychiatrists or specialized physicians?

Yes. If specialized psychiatric care is required, we are happy to provide professional referrals to ensure you receive the appropriate level of support.

Do you provide emergency or crisis management services?

Our services are designed for non-emergency support only. We do not offer crisis management or 24/7 emergency intervention. For immediate danger or life-threatening situations, please contact local emergency services.

What is your refund policy?

All payments made are final and non-refundable. We encourage you to reach out with any questions you may have before completing your purchase to ensure our services are the right fit for your needs.

Mindfulness, CBT, & EFT

We integrate evidence-based techniques to give you practical tools for daily life.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT focuses on identifying and challenging negative thought patterns. By understanding how your thoughts influence your feelings and behaviors, you can learn to reframe them. It is highly effective for depression, anxiety, and stress management.

Mindfulness

Mindfulness is the practice of being present in the moment without judgment. Through breathwork and awareness exercises, you learn to observe your thoughts rather than being swept away by them. This reduces reactivity and cultivates a sense of calm.

Emotional Freedom Techniques (EFT)

Also known as "tapping," EFT combines cognitive therapy with somatic stimulation of acupressure points. It is a powerful tool for quickly reducing emotional intensity, stress, and cravings.

Other Psychotherapies

One size does not fit all in therapy. We draw from a diverse range of therapeutic modalities to create a personalized plan for you.

Depending on your needs, we may incorporate:

  • Humanistic Therapy: Focusing on self-discovery and personal growth.
  • Psychodynamic Therapy: Exploring how past experiences influence current behavior.
  • Solution-Focused Brief Therapy (SFBT): Concentrating on finding solutions in the present rather than analyzing the past.
  • Art & Expressive Therapies: Using creativity to access emotions that are hard to put into words.

We work collaboratively with you to find the approach that feels most comfortable and effective for your unique journey.
